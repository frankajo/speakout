About Us

The SpeakOut Organisation has been helping victims of sexual and domestic violence for over 40 years. After four decades of service, the WRC is known as the areaâ€™s primary service provider for domestic and sexual violence survivors, assisting residential and non-residential clients with support, advocacy and immediate crisis intervention. The agency is also the acknowledged area expert on sexual assault, domestic violence, dating violence, and stalking and annually provides approximately 150 public awareness, prevention, education and awareness activities for allied professionals and agencies, civic groups and schools.

SpeakOut is a collection of professional service provisers and official that respond as a group and in a timely fashion to various needs of domestic violence and sexual violence survivors by providing legal, financial, medical, counselling and psychological support.

It is to encourage any individual who is passing through any form of domestic or sexual violence to SpeakUp.

Our Vision

SpeakOut vision is that every community in which we serve can take pride in having safe homes and safe relationships.

We are committed to provide help and hope to all who have been deprived of these basic human rights and we are dedicated to work collaboratively to build strong, positive, nonviolent communities.

Our Mission

"To create a society in which domestic violence no longer exists.
We seek to prevent domestic and sexual violence by advocating for positive changes in the societal systems that may lead to oppression, fostering a better understanding of domestic and sexual violence through our different programs, and providing culturally relevant direct services that empower victims of abuse"
